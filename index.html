About Me

Daniel Stone

Since I was a child, I had a knack for tinkering with technologies (stemming from a broken clock - which I fixed with extra parts!). At the University of Southern California, I learned about web development which further piqued my interest in the subject of programming. I continued to broaden my knowledge by taking ventures in IT administration, project and program manangement. At LearningFuze, I was treated to the fundamentals of web development. I am continuously learning and creating, fixing, refining processes and streamlining for more efficiency. And my journey continues...
