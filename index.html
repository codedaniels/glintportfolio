About Me

Daniel Stone

Since I was a child, I had a knack for tinkering with technologies (stemming from a broken clock - which I fixed with extra parts!). While in high school there was an elective course for a programming language, Pascal, and that sparked my interest in programming. At the University of Southern California, I learned about web development which further piqued my interest in the subject of programming. Life took a detour of sorts where I dabbled in IT administration, project and program manangement. I thoroughly enjoy continuously learning new things, fixing broken things, creating new things, refining processes and streamlining for more efficiency. At LearningFuze, I was treated to the fundamentals of web development. And my journey continues...
